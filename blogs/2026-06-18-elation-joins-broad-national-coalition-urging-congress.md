Elation Health joined over 260 organizations urging Congress to fully fund the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) at $500 million for FY27. The coalition emphasizes that AHRQ's research on healthcare delivery systems is essential for improving patient outcomes, strengthening primary care in underserved communities, and making care safer and more affordable.
